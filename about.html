About Us

Your dog will enjoy its walk with a small group of friendly, happy dogs and come home ready to relax, allowing you to enjoy your evening knowing your dog has been out exercising and having fun with his or her friends. Waggles & Whiskers only walks small groups of well-behaved dogs to ensure everyone is safe and gets the care and attention they deserve.

Waggles & Whiskers is fully insured and I am registered with Brighton and Hove Council as a Professional Dog Walker.

I have a canine first aid certificate, am DBS (police) checked and have completed the City and Guilds Course for Professional Dog Walkers for which I was awarded a distinction.

At university I studied animal physiology and behaviour as part of my Biology degree course, I love getting to know the individual dogs personalities and enjoy watching their interactions, I find them fascinating and often amusing. I also have a longstanding interest in environmental issues and animal health and welfare. Waggles & Whiskers uses biodegradable pooh bags and environmentally friendly cleaning products to help reduce the impact on our environment. I currently drive a small unleaded petrol van but will be switching to electric in the future.

Having walked dogs for many years, kept pets all of my life and looked after many different animals you can rest assured yours is in safe and caring hands.

Your dog will be picked up and dropped off in Waggles & Whiskers specially adapted air conditioned / heated van which has been inspected and approved by Brighton & Hove Council. The van is fully equipped with individual crates lined with cosy fleecy blankets and/or towels depending on the weather ensuring your dog is comfortable and safe on the journey. I also have a portable shower and lots of towels in the van to wash and dry muddy paws and always carry plenty of fresh drinking water and a first aid kit.

Cat feeding and pet visits are available in the Hanover and Elm Grove area and will last approximately 20 minutes allowing time for cuddles and play as well as feeding and cleaning the litter tray if required.

Please text me, send an email or give me a call to discuss your pet and his or her requirements. We can then arrange a convenient time for me to visit and meet you and your pet.
