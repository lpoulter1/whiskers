Your dog will enjoy its walk with a small group of friendly, happy dogs and come home ready to relax. Waggles & Whiskers walks a maximum of four dogs at a time to ensure each one gets the care and attention they deserve. Individual walks can be arranged to suit you and your dog.

Waggles & Whiskers is fully insured and I am registered with Brighton and Hove Council as a Professional Dog Walker.

I have a canine first aid certificate, am DBS (police) checked and currently completing the City and Guilds Course for Professional Dog Walkers.

At university I studied animal physiology and behaviour as part of my Biology degree course. I have a longstanding interest in environmental issues and animal health and welfare. Waggles & Whiskers uses biodegradable pooh bags and environmentally friendly cleaning products to help reduce the impact on our environment.

Having kept pets all of my life and looked after many different animals you can rest assured your pet is in safe and caring hands.
