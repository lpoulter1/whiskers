Waggles & Whiskers provides professional, reliable and friendly dog walking, cat feeding and pet services to suit your needs.

Whether you’re at work, out for the day or on holiday you can be sure your pets will be happy, healthy and safe with Waggles & Whiskers.

Dogs can be walked with others for fun and socialisation or exercised alone if preferred. Walks are varied and chosen to provide excitement and mental stimulation for your dog, reducing boredom and destructive behaviours. Stanmer Park and Sheepcote Valley are some of our favourite places to explore. Bespoke individual walks can also be arranged for a more personal service if required.

Cats and other pets can be visited once or twice a day for feeding and play as required and will be cared for with love while you’re away.
