Waggles & Whiskers is a well-established and reputable company based in the Hanover / Elm Grove area of Brighton that provides a professional, reliable and friendly dog walking service that you can trust.

Whether you’re busy working at home or out for the day you can be sure your dog will be happy, healthy and safe with Waggles & Whiskers.

Your dog will benefit both physically and mentally from socialising and playing with other friendly dogs on group walks. Walks are in safe open spaces away from roads in places especially chosen to provide excitement and mental stimulation for your dog. Walks are varied and usually include some time in woodland and also in open fields, they are carefully planned to suit the particular dogs and weather conditions at the time. Group walks are great fun for the dogs and can help reduce boredom, anxiety and destructive behaviours at home. They also learn valuable social skills whilst out with their friends. Your dog will come home deeply relaxed and sleep soundly after their walk.

Cats and other pets in the Hanover / Elm Grove area can be visited once or twice a day for feeding and play as required and will be cared for with love while you’re away.
